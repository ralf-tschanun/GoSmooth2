Welcome to GoSmooth

Are you a good driver?
Of course you are! But let’s find out together.
Let GoSmooth use your iPhone to measure how good you really are.

The GPS data and motion sensors of your iPhone are continuously analyzed by the GoSmooth AI. Every maneuver is evaluated, and a total score is calculated — giving you a neutral, objective assessment of your driving performance.

75% of all drivers consider themselves above average.
With GoSmooth, you can find out whether you are truly a good driver — or maybe even a better one.
